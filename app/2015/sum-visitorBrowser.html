What OS and browser are our visitors using?

Key take aways

Even though we are a Mac based company, our visitors are not. The biggest question is, are we doing enough testing to insure our Windows experience is solid? Also, are we doing a great job with our IE compatibility?

It's a Windows World

Approximately 75% of the head traffic is running a Windows variant. This obviously constitutes an enormous share of our monthly traffic and highlights two important design/development factors; layout and development. If we doing a poor job of experiences for these visitors we will impact revenue at a much higher rate.

Chrome Dominates, But IE Holds On

The latest Chrome browser(s) dominates the traffic, but IE holds strong because IE 8, 9, 10 & 11 still appear in the top 10. So in total, IE still is our second strongest browser and unfortunately probably our most under targeted.

Note: These values are an aggregation of all similar OSs from the top 10 occurrences. These values do not represent the absolute numbers of all Operating Systems, just the head.
